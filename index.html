Print-your-own piano helper template

  1. Print this page
  2. Cut out both templates
  3. Fold on dotted lines marked FOLD
  4. Identify a section of keyboard with 2 black keys before 3 black keys → 
  5. Place larger cutout over this part of keyboard
  6. Insert folded flaps into space between keys
  7. Place smaller cutout on top of larger cutout
  8. Simultaneously press keys designated by the dots to play chords
