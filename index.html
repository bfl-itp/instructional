How To Cast Your Hand

List of materials:
  • Alja-Safe Alginate 3-lb Box
  • Hydrocal White Gypsum Cement (less than 5lbs needed, for smaller amounts check your local art store)
  • Mixing buckets and measuring cups of various sizes
  • Wood mixing sticks
  • Pitcher
  • Water
  • A model hand
Mix the alginate with water
1:1 ratio (about 40oz of each)
Immediately pour alginate into pitcher
Immediately place hand in liquid alginate
Hold in place for 8min as alginate sets
Gently remove hand from mold
Prepare the plaster
  • Sift plaster into water and let sit untouched about 2min
  • Ratio is 3 parts plaster (about 2.5lbs) : 1 part water (about 13oz)
Vigorously mix the plaster in a circular motion
Be weary of air bubbles
